About Our Application

The world shutting down and everyone sitting at home, gave a huge rise to content creation . Many onboarded the trends. With the rise of content creators came a rise of consumers as well. The pandemic proved that there are never "too many creators". Everyone can be a creator and a consumer . Not everyone has the time and money to make awesome looking content. We believe creators should focus on their content and not have to focus on setting up their lights, to be well heard , adjusting their cameras, or be super conscious of one-self . That is why we wanted to create a platform where seamlessly anyone could become an awesome creator. So we use " Deep Fake " technology to achieve the above. Deep fake is a type of artificial intelligence used to create convincing images, audio and video hoaxes.
